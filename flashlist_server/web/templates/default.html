How to Delete Your Account

  1. Please open the app and navigate to the settings page.
  2. The last item in the list of settings will be "Delete Account".
  3. Click on this item, read the warning, and confirm your choice.

Please Note: All Flashlists that you created will be deleted with your account. Even if there are others listed as Editors

If you have any questions please reach out via ben@flashlistapp.com. Thank you for trying Flashlist!
